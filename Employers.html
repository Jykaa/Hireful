WHY HIREFUL?


Hireful is the new online way of applying and posting jobs. It is fast, easy, and secure. Hirefull allows you to link many of your social networks like Linkedin, github and facebook. You dont have to waste time looking for employees, tehy will look for you!

HOW DOES IT WORK?


Employers can post jobs on hireful and then get apllications in very short time. The amount of jobs you can post depends on your employers level. Once you post your jobs, all you have to do is wait for applicants. Once you recieve the apllications, you can book interview in person or hireful offers online interview with just a clik of a button. Once you find the right person for the job, you can click the "position filled" button and the job is automatically taken down and applications are closed.
