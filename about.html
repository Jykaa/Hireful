What Are We?

Hireful is an interactive platform designed to match the best candidates to the best software jobs. Here, we believe in professional match-making functions, similar to personal ones. So, this site is an interactive and personal touch into the hiring process.

Our Origins

Hireful was created and developed in end 2018 for the SEG2900 class at uOttawa, aiming to create a novel and effective start-up pitch. All members, then, decided to innovate in the talent acquisiton market, as it has seen few innovations and market shifts in the past decade.

Our Platform

Hireful is a cross-platform program, and works on both the web and mobile. It is now possible to connect to applicants for companies using an interactive, dating style, service maximising interaction and prioritizing only the information that matters.

Visions for The Future

The future is one where the hiring process is more personal and engaging, while also streamlining it so only the most compatible companies and applicants meet. This will all be possible, thanks to Hireful.
