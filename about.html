What Are We?

We provide an interactive platform to match the best candidates to the best software jobs. We believe professional match-making functions similar to personal ones, so we have implemented an interactive and personal touch into the hiring process.

Our Origins

We were developed for our SEG2900 class, aiming to create a novel and effective start-up pitch. We decided to innovate in the talen acquisiton market, as it has seen few innovations and market shifts in the past decade.

Our Platform

We are a cross-platform program on both the web and mobile, connecting applicants to companies using an interactive, dating style service maximising interaction and prioritizing only the information that matters.

Visions for The Future

Our vision of the future is one where the hiring process is more personal and engaging, while also streamlining it so only the most compatible companies and applicants meet.
